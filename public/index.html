Image hosting

By default, posters are attached to each email as inline (CID) images. Most clients render them inline, but Apple Mail, iOS Mail, and Outlook also list ~30 of them as attachments. Hosting posters on Cloudinary (free tier is plenty) keeps the email lightweight — each poster is uploaded once, cached, and embedded as a regular <img src="https://…">.

Find these in Cloudinary under Settings → API Keys. Posters are deduplicated by Plex rating_key + thumb version, so each poster is only uploaded once.

Email (SMTP)

Pivo works with any SMTP provider — Postmark, Brevo, Mailgun, SendGrid, Amazon SES, or your own self-hosted relay. Grab the host + port + credentials from your provider's dashboard.

For best deliverability, set up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC DNS records on your sending domain in your SMTP provider's dashboard. Without these, Gmail and Yahoo will route your emails to spam.

Public URL (for unsubscribe links)

Used to build per-recipient unsubscribe links and the List-Unsubscribe header (one-click compliance for Gmail / Yahoo). Leave blank to omit unsubscribe links — fine for a tiny private list, not recommended for anything bigger. No trailing slash, e.g. https://newsletter.yourdomain.com.
